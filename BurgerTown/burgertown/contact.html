About Fatty Burger

Welcome to Fatty Burger, where the art of burger-making meets a passion for flavor. Since our inception in 1998, we've been on a relentless journey to redefine the burger experience. Our commitment to quality ingredients and culinary innovation has made Fatty Burger a destination for food enthusiasts.

At Fatty Burger, we take pride in our signature Shack Burger. Crafted with a double layer of sear-sizzled 100% pure beef, special sauce, and topped with melty American cheese, crisp lettuce, minced onions, and tangy pickles, each bite is a celebration of taste.

Our chefs are more than cooks; they are artists, meticulously creating burgers that go beyond the ordinary. We source the finest ingredients, ensuring that every burger tells a story of quality and freshness. From our classic house-ground hamburger served in a grilled rosemary focaccia to our Cheddar Junky Stuffed Burgers, our menu is a testament to culinary excellence.

But Fatty Burger is more than a restaurant; it's a community. We believe in giving back, supporting local initiatives, and creating a welcoming space for families and friends. Join us on this gastronomic journey, and let Fatty Burger be your go-to destination for burger perfection.
